• This is the question I asked myself at the end of the summer.
  • I'm the software guy for a student group called StartLabs.
  • We give engineering students the resources to start companies.
  • We also encourage kids to intern at startups over the summer to see if they like the lifestyle.
  • Thought it made sense to provide a jobs list as a service to the MIT community.
  • So I set out to build it. Here were the goals I had in mind...
  • Balancing act between exposing powerful functionality while maintaining ease-of-use, both for students browsing and for companies submitting.
  • And have fields that make sense in the context of startups. I want to know how large the company is. Location is important.
